Introduction

Jane Austen was an English novelist known for her sharp wit, social commentary, and timeless characters. Her most famous works, including Pride and Prejudice, Sense and Sensibility, and Emma, explore themes of love, class, and independence in the lives of women during the early 19th century.

Her Most Famous Works

  1. Pride and Prejudice
    • Published in 1813
    • Main characters: Elizabeth Bennet & Mr. Darcy
    • Hertfordshire, Derbyshire, Kent
  2. Sense and Sensibility
    • Published in 1811
    • Main characters: Elinor & Marianne Dashwood
    • Sussex, Devonshire, London
  3. Emma
    • Published in 1815
    • Main character: Emma Woodhouse
    • Village of Highbury
